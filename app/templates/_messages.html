Gas Pumps

This single-faceted user interface consist of Sections organized into Rows, the customer simply needs to select the desired option in each Row. A Linear flow user experience is the most intuitive and minimalist experience a machine can ever offer.


Mobile Application

The UI/UX design for the Mobile application is just like the design for the gas Pump, this is to ensure a consistent user experience throughout platforms. To buy gas the user simply needs to select the desired fuel type, specify desired amount, select payment method and scan the QR pump or pair the mobile device with the Pump.


Vehicle Dashboard App

Vehicles can also download this mobile app for their dashboard, users could also buy fuel via their vehicles dashboard. To Buy gas the user submit his order and the vehicle pairs with the gas pump near it.



Send/Request Fuel

A particular feature consumers will love is the ability to seamlessly Send/Request fuel to/from friends and family just like they would send/request cash via their banking app.



TapBar Buttons

From the Tapbar, in the mobile application, the user can seamlessly access the screens he/she will be using the most: The map to find gas stations, the Main screen to buy fuel, and the notifications view receipt of previous purchases and more.



Linear-Number-Pad

Because the main goal of this design is to enable customers to seamlessly buy fuel, the entire user experience was designed around the numpad. Because the Number-Pad plays such an important role it deserves to be permanently displayed on the screen and in order for it to be permanently in the screen it most be minimalist and thats why it its linear because this format occupies less space then the traditional square shaped numpad.

About

The project Easy Pay is a Mobile Application designed for everyday self-service of buying fuel at gas stations and more.


This project is the beginning of a new much simpler era where customers wont ever have to experience long queues at at the filling stations.


Because innovation is about making life simpler we can anticipate a non-distant future where the Easy Pay Application would become a mandatory standard in everyday devices and mobile applications.


This minimalist concept ensures a very simple, intuitive, fluent user experience for customers of any age, gender, religion, etc. It is so Intuitive it does not even needs to guide the customer through the process of submitting an order.


The goal of this project ultimately is to make the customer experience in everyday self-service fueling machine pump and a mobile application as simple, seamless and efficient as possible. The UI/UX design in our mobile application is more then 90% responsible for the customer experience.



Customers will enjoy a simplified more intuitive experience that will allow them to self-service themselves more seamlessly and faster.



This design concept is a game changer for many important and prestigious petrochemical entities who are always looking for innovations that help them better serve their customers.
